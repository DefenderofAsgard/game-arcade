Orbit Mechanics

Every level launches you from a different planet in the solar system, each with its own real surface gravity — the same speed and angle won't work twice. You get 3 attempts per level and 60 seconds to decide on each one — if time runs out, that attempt is used up. Set a launch angle & speed, then hit Launch — straight up just falls back down, so aim sideways to actually get into orbit, the same way real rockets pitch over after liftoff. Too slow and you'll fall back into the planet; too fast and you'll fly off into space. Keep your orbit from exceeding the outer edge of the target ring for a full revolution — tighter rings are worth more points. Run out of attempts on a level and the session ends.
